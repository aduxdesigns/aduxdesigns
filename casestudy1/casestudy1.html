Immigration Practice Management SaaS

(in adherence to NDA, client name & logo are replaced)

Overview

Immigration Practice Management is an AI-enabled, cloud-based platform designed for immigration law firms and legal professionals. It centralizes case management, workflows, documentation, client communication, and compliance within a secure system.

Problem

Immigration attorneys, paralegals, practice managers, and IT & compliance teams currently rely on multiple disconnected tools for case tracking, deadlines, documentation, and communication—resulting in operational inefficiencies, fragmented data, compliance risks, and missed deadlines.

Goal

  • Streamline workflows: Manage end-to-end cases in one system, reducing handoffs and tool switching.
  • Improve visibility: Provide real-time case status, deadlines, and task ownership.
  • Reduce compliance risk: Ensure security and compliance through role-based access and audit trails.
  • Centralize data: Create a single source of truth for cases, documents, and communication.
  • Prevent missed deadlines: Use automated alerts and reminders for critical timelines.
  • Enhance collaboration: Enable secure, seamless communication across legal teams.

Discovery & Define

We collaborated to explore user needs and product requirements for building a new immigration software solution from the ground up. This digital showcase highlights the key insights, ideation, and decisions that shaped the foundation of the product experience.



Workflow Analysis

As part of the design process, I defined the overall project flow and created detailed, role-based sub-flows that mapped key tasks and interactions. This helped ensure clarity across features and enabled users to complete workflows smoothly and efficiently.



Competitor Research

Competitor analysis of Docketwise, LollyLaw, Filevine, Imagility, and INSZoom revealed strong capabilities in form automation, compliance, and case tracking, but with complex and fragmented workflows. This identified an opportunity to design a more centralized, role-based experience that improves clarity, efficiency, and ease of use.


Design & Deliver

The design objective for the Immigration Management application is to deliver an intuitive, user-focused experience that simplifies navigation, improves data visualization, and ensures accessibility for all users.

Digitizing Employee & Employer Questionnaires

Converted all Employee and Employer questionnaires from static documents into structured, interactive e-forms, reducing manual effort, errors, and back-and-forth communication.


Dashboard

Designed a centralized dashboard that eliminates the need to navigate between multiple sections or screens, enabling users to view high-priority data, track end-to-end operations, and take action from a single interface.


Case Details

A centralized Case Details page that allows all user roles to access relevant information, perform role-specific tasks, and update case status and activities in one place.


Other Designs

Design System

Product Website

Designed and delivered a complete marketing website including Features, Pricing, Insights, About, and Contact pages, ensuring a cohesive visual language and clear user journeys across the product.


Mobile App

Designed a cloud-integrated mobile experience that allows users to securely review, assign, and approve cases on the go. The solution supports both Android and iOS, reducing turnaround time and minimizing delays when users are away from their desks.
